Prototyping Process

This method introduces you to prototyping, a process consisting of several steps to ultimately build your own level from scratch.

Process Steps

  1. Understand what you want to create
  2. Create diagrams and flowcharts
  3. Draw a map
  4. Get a Sense of Space
  5. Iterate on the Map
  6. Blockout
  7. Iterate
